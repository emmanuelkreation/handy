Our story — from a small workshop to Lagos's trusted furniture maker

Handygidi started as a tiny furniture repair shop in Surulere. We saw that most “luxury” furniture in Lagos was overpriced and under-quality. So we decided to build our own.

Today, we have a full production workshop in Lekki, employing skilled carpenters, upholsterers, and finishers. Every sofa, bed, and table is made here, using solid wood and premium materials. No imports. No shortcuts.

We are proud to serve families, businesses, and real estate developers across Lagos – with furniture that lasts.
