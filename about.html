Our story — from a frustrating search to a movement

Handygidi started when our founder, Chidi, spent months trying to find a designer who truly listened. Most offered pre‑made templates or overpriced generic solutions. He thought: “Lagos deserves better.”

So he built Handygidi – a design studio that blends African warmth with modern elegance. Today, we’ve helped over 500 families and businesses fall in love with their spaces again. No arrogance. No hidden fees. Just genuine care and stunning results.
